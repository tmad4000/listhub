The Agent-First Web

Every page is an API

If a human can do it, an agent can do it. No separate "developer" experience.

Your agent, everywhere

A persistent sidebar or bar that follows you across the web. Not per-app AI — your personal agent.

Self-service onboarding

Agents register themselves. No OAuth flows, no approval queues. One POST, one key, go.

Context-aware actions

The agent sees what page you're on and suggests relevant actions. "Add this to your list?"
